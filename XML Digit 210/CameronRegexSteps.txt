Regex Steps for Movie list

Find '&' 

Replace all '&' with '&amp;'

Find "'^.+" to highlight each row individually

Replace with '<movie>\0</movie>' to tag the entire line as a movie description as a whole

Find '(<movie>)(.+?)\t' to get the information that is the first column of information in the series

Replace with '\1<title>\2</title>' in order to tag that first column as the title section of data

</title>\d+?\t find digits that are after title end tag

\1<year>\2</year>

(<time)(>\d+)\s+(min)

\1 unit="\3"\2

(</country>)(.+?)(</movie>)

\1<time>\2</time>\3
